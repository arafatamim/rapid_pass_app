# Privacy Policy for Amar Rapid Pass

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

## Overview
Amar Rapid Pass is an independent, unofficial client for viewing Rapid Pass account and card information. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Dhaka Transport Coordination Authority (DTCA) or the Rapid Pass service.

The app does not operate its own backend servers. When you use the app, it communicates directly with third-party services that you choose to use, mainly the official Rapid Pass website.

## Data We Handle
Depending on the features you use, the app may handle the following data on your device:

- Rapid Pass login identifier such as your email address or mobile number
- Rapid Pass password
- Session cookies returned by the Rapid Pass website
- Rapid Pass account, card, balance, and transaction information returned by the Rapid Pass website
- NFC card identifiers and NFC card history read from a physical Rapid Pass-compatible card
- Basic app settings such as whether you accepted the in-app disclaimer

## Where Data Is Stored
The app stores data locally on your device.

- Login identifiers and passwords are stored in encrypted storage on Android
- Saved account snapshots, card data, and linked NFC snapshots are stored locally on your device
- The app developer does not receive or store this data on a separate Amar Rapid Pass server

## How Data Is Used
The app uses your data only to provide the features you ask it to perform, including:

- Signing in to the official Rapid Pass website on your behalf
- Fetching and displaying your saved cards, balances, and transaction history
- Saving account data locally so you do not need to re-enter credentials every time
- Reading NFC card data when you choose to scan a physical card
- Linking NFC scan results to a saved card on your device
- Checking for newer app versions

## Third-Party Services
Amar Rapid Pass may communicate with the following third-party services:

- `https://rapidpass.com.bd` for Rapid Pass sign-in, account, card, and transaction data
- Google Play, when the Android build is distributed through Google Play and the app checks store version information
- GitHub release metadata for non-Play update checks on supported non-Android platforms

These services have their own privacy practices and policies.

## What We Do Not Do
The Amar Rapid Pass developer does not:

- run a separate backend that stores your Amar Rapid Pass data
- sell your personal data
- use your data for advertising
- use analytics or ad SDKs in this app

## Security
We take reasonable steps within the app to reduce unnecessary exposure of your data:

- credentials are stored using encrypted storage on Android
- data is stored locally rather than on an Amar Rapid Pass server

No software or local storage approach can guarantee absolute security. You should only use the app on a device you trust.

## Data Retention
Your locally stored Amar Rapid Pass data remains on your device until you remove it.

- Removing a saved account from inside the app deletes the saved login, saved card snapshots, and linked NFC data associated with that saved account from this device
- Uninstalling the app removes the app from your device, but platform backup or retention behavior may depend on your operating system
- Deleting an Amar Rapid Pass account from the official Rapid Pass service must be done through the Rapid Pass service itself, not through the Amar Rapid Pass developer

## Account and Data Deletion
Instructions for deleting Amar Rapid Pass local data and requesting deletion of your Rapid Pass account are published here:

## Children's Privacy
The app is not directed to children and is not intended for use by children under 13.
