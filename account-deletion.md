# Account and Data Deletion for Amar Rapid Pass

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

## Amar Rapid Pass Local Data
Amar Rapid Pass stores account data locally on your device so it can show your saved Rapid Pass cards and NFC links.

You can delete that local Amar Rapid Pass data at any time:

1. Open Amar Rapid Pass.
2. Go to `Settings`.
3. Open `Manage saved accounts on this device`.
4. Remove the saved account you want to delete from this device.

Removing a saved account from the app deletes the locally stored:

- saved login identifier
- saved password
- saved card snapshots
- linked NFC card snapshots associated with that saved account

This local deletion only affects data stored by Amar Rapid Pass on your device.

## Rapid Pass Account Deletion
Your actual Rapid Pass account is provided by the official Rapid Pass service, not by Amar Rapid Pass.

To delete or request deletion of your Rapid Pass account:

1. Sign in to the official Rapid Pass website.
2. Review the account/profile controls made available by the service, including the profile page exposed by the service at:
   `https://rapidpass.com.bd/user/edit-profile`
3. If your account does not expose a direct delete option, use the official Rapid Pass website privacy, help, or support channels to request account deletion:
   `https://rapidpass.com.bd/privacy-policy`

The Amar Rapid Pass developer cannot directly delete operator-managed Rapid Pass accounts or operator-side records.
